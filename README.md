## 🎯 Project Overview

Comprehensive Exploratory Data Analysis on **15,000 retail transactions** across 3 years (2022–2024) covering 5 product categories and 7 cities. Identifies seasonal patterns, top products, and actionable business recommendations.

---

| Metric | Value |

| Total Revenue | ₹290.1M (3 years) |
| Total Profit | ₹80M |
| Overall Margin | 27.6% |
| Top Category | Electronics |
| Peak Month | November (Festive season) |
| Top City | Bangalore |
| Top Product | Headphones |

---

## 🔍 Key Findings

1. **Electronics drives 32% of revenue** but Clothing has the highest margin (48%)
2. **October–December = Festive spike** — revenue jumps 35% above annual average
3. **Weekends outperform weekdays** — 15% higher average transaction value
4. **Bangalore leads revenue** — 23% of total sales across all cities
5. **UPI is the dominant payment** — 38% of all transactions

---

## 📊 Business Recommendations

1. **Festive season inventory** — Stock up Electronics + Clothing 45 days before Oct
2. **City expansion** — Replicate Bangalore's high-value customer acquisition in Hyderabad
3. **Margin optimization** — Promote Clothing (48% margin) over Electronics (22% margin)
4. **Weekend campaigns** — Schedule promotions for Saturday-Sunday to capture peak intent
